TEST 3: CUSTOMER PLAN UPGRADE
------------------------------
1. Sign in as customer (not salon owner)
2. Go to: http://localhost:3000/rewards
3. See current membership tier (Basic/Premium/VIP)
4. Click "Upgrade to Premium" or "Upgrade to VIP" card
   OR
   Click the upgrade banner at the top
5. Payment modal opens showing:
   - Plan name and price
   - Payment method selection (UPI/QR)
   - Amount to pay
6. Select payment method:
   - UPI: Copy merchant UPI ID (7507075722@mbk)
         Open any UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm)
         Pay to the UPI ID
         Return and enter 12-digit transaction ID
   - QR: Scan QR code with UPI app
         Pay the amount
         Enter 12-digit transaction ID
7. Click "Verify Payment"
8. Success! Membership upgraded
9. Notification shows in bell icon
10. Page refreshes, tier badge updated

✅ Expected: Payment modal opens, payment verifies, membership upgrades
